How do coupons work?

Use NEXUS20 for 20% off subtotal or FIRST50 for flat ₹50 (capped).

Where is data stored?

Browser localStorage for the SPA demo. C++ modules persist to text files under backend/data/.

Can I connect a database?

Yes — replace file persistence with SQLite or a REST API; OOP layer stays the same.
